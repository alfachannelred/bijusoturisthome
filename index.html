Welcome to Biju's Tourist Home...

Kochi, Kerala, India.
Biju’s Tourist Home has been established in 1981 by ‘ KANJIRAKAT THACHIL ‘ family. The location , being the centre of Ernakulam, makes us an ideal destination for the business traveller and the tourist.Our excellent services backed by a trained professional team makes the stay comfortable. Many International travel guides have mentioned our team’s gracious hospitality and our hotel as , one of the best at its range. We maintain a personal rapport with all the guests, constantly receiving feed back from them and using this knowledge to improve our service. We help you to reach out to the places which enriches the heritage of kerala like ‘Jewish Synagogue ‘, The Dutch Palace,The ‘Bolgatty Palace’, The Chinese fishing nets, The ancient temples and churches. We guide you to see the lively cultural activity ‘Kathakali’ one of the traditional dance form of kerala.We also make arrangements for boat cruise along the canals and lagoons for you to enjoy the scenic beauty of Kochi.
Biju’s Tourist Home has been a regular camp for many groups of tourist from all over the world. International groups like ‘ Ashraf Travels ‘ – Holland, ‘ Dragoman ‘ – U.K., ‘ Encounter’ – U.K., ‘ Exodus ‘ – U.K.,’ Nouvells’ Frontiers – France, ‘ Neiza – Thakikon ‘ – Israel, ‘Adeo’ – France, ‘ ‘ ‘Window to the world’ – Australia etc. are our regular customers for the past two decades. We do our best in every way we can , to give you an enjoyable time in the ‘ Gods own Country ‘.
